New Blockchain Technology

Our main goal is to allow our users to make quick and reliable cryptocurrency transfers without any fees.

More...

Instead of the standard blockchain we have a DAG (Directed Acyclic Graph), which we call Tangle. Transactions represent a set of tangle nodes. Its edge set is formed as follows: when it receives a new transaction, it must approve two previous transactions by solving a cryptographic puzzle similar to the one that is contained in the bitcoin-miner.

POM - Proofof Me

After each transaction there will be an emission of tokens, which will be returned to your wallet through cashback.

More...

Since the network users act as minors, allocating power of their own computers and phones, it is only fair to reward them for each transaction they make. Once a month a certain number of tokens is distributed among the network participants (the more transactions one has made, the more tokens he gets at the end of a month).

Smart contracts

We believe that purchase and sale of cryptocurrencies should be as convenient as possible. All cryptocurrency transactions should be made without losses of one’s own financial resources. We are happy to provide our users with such opportunities.

More...

Exchanges usually take high fees for transactions, while in Paymon there will be no intermediary. There will be a system of smart contracts within the platform, which will connect all of the users willing to buy or sell cryptocurrency with each other.

How it works:

Alice joins Paymon and wants to buy 1 bitcoin.

She sends an order for 1 bitcoin to the system.

Paymon system starts searching for a seller.

At the same time Bob wants to sell a bitcoin.

The system connects them and the exchange takes place, while both of them have no idea who is on the other end of the transaction. It all happens within the system in an instant, without any fees.

LEGAL SUPPORT

Nowadays there are a lot of scam projects, so it is important to provide all of the ICO participants with some guarantees and protection.

More...

After the ICO each investor will be able to receive an electronic copy of a document, that confirms the fact of investment. It is a kind of a contract offer which has legal weight in some jurisdictions. The program will draw up the contract itself, it will only require a small piece of information about the investor and an e-mail where the document will be sent to. This legal paper will contain transaction elements, identification of the parties, as well as their obligations. It will indicate that you have invested in the project a crypto-asset in the form of BTC or ETH, and that the company is obliged to give you its equivalent in PMT.
