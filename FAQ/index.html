Is there a commission when exchange cryptocurrency?

With each transaction, you yourself have the right to set and determine what commission you are willing to pay for the transaction. The more you put the commission, the faster the translation speed. This function is available only for Bitcoin.

  • Bitcoin:

    • - The recommended minimum commission for the transfer of Bitcoin is 0.0005 Btc.

  • Ethereum:

    • - The Commission depends on the network load and usually not more than 525000 Gwei (~0.18$).

How to make a transaction in Paymon?

  • By registering with Paymon, you immediately create and attach crypto-currency wallets to your account.
  • These wallets are in the blocking system and only you have access to them.
  • After making fiat money, the system immediately converts them into a crypto currency, you can dispose of it.
  • You can make transfers through the "money" tab in the application, you need to know the purse address or in the system Paymon you can find your friend in the search, open a dialogue with him and in the upper right corner click on the transfer button and the system will understand who exactly you send money and recognize your friend's address.

Input and Output.

Important!

  • Input and output is carried out only in the ways indicated in Paymon, we strongly insist that you do not look for additional intermediaries in order to avoid losing your funds.
  • In order to deposit funds you need to go to the "money" tab in the mobile application and click on the "refill"
  • To output, respectively, click the "withdrawal of funds".
  • Commission for input and output is determined already before the transaction itself and it is individual for each order.

Can I merge existing cryptoccurencies wallets?

  • You can not have two purses of one currency, but you can load third-party wallets into Paymon.

    • - For Ethereum, the JSON format.

How to make a wallet cold? What is a cold wallet?

  • For Bitcoin purse, this function is not available.
  • For the Etherium purse, you can make a backup purse and in the application click the "delete" button after that your purse will become cold.

    • A cold purse allows you to store electronic currencies on a physical medium. It works without access to the Internet, which means that attackers can not reach it.

How do you protect my communications with others?

  • The application uses our proprietary Protocol. All traffic is encrypted with algorithms Diffie-Hellman (Diffie-Hellman), AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) using a checksum SHA (Secure Hash Algorithm). All correspondence encrypted with the algorithm RSA-2048.
