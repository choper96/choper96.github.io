NEW BLOCKCHAIN-TECHNOLOGY

Our main mission is to create comfortable conditions for our users to transfer the crypto currency. This should happen quickly and reliably, and most importantly, without paying commissions.

More...

Instead of the standard blockchain, there is a DAG (directed acyclic graph), which we call tangle. Transactions are a set of tangle nodes. Its edge set is obtained as follows: upon receipt of a new transaction, it must approve the two previous transactions. In order for a transaction to be valid, the node must solve a cryptographic puzzle similar to the one contained in the bitcoin-miner

POM - proof of me

When making a transaction, there will be a micro-emission of tokens, it will be returned to your wallet in the form of cashback.

More...

Since miners of the network are the users themselves, which allocate the power of computers and telephones, it is fair to reward them for each transaction they had made. Once a month, a certain number of tokens are evenly distributed among the network participants (the more transactions the user has made, the greater part of the tokens he will receive at the end of the month).

Smart contracts

We believe that the purchase and sale of cryptocurrencies should be as convenient as possible. All operations with the cryptocurrency should take place without loss of own financial resources. We are happy to provide this opportunity to our users.

More...

How it works:

Alice enters into Paymon and wants to buy 1 bitcoin.

Alice creates an order and sends an order to buy 1 bitcoin into the system.

The Paymon system starts searching for a buyer.

Bob at this point wants to sell 1 Bitcoin.

The system synchronizes them and there is an exchange between them, all this is done inside the system, and neither Alice nor Bob knows who is the sender and who is the recipient and all this happens instantly and without excessive commissions.

LEGAL SUPPORT

At the time of scam-projects, it is necessary to give an element of protection and guarantees for the participants of the ICO.

More...

After attending the ICO, you are entitled to receive an electronic copy of the document confirming the fact of investing. This is a kind of contract of an offer, which has legal weight, in some jurisdictions. You just specify your details and the email address to which you want to get the contract. The rest will be done by the program. In this document, the details of the parties to the transaction, the element of the transaction and the responsibilities of each party will be prescribed. This document will indicate that you have invested in us a crypto-asset in the form of BTC or ETH, and we, for our part, are obliged to issue the equivalent of the money invested in your funds PMT.
